# INTENT: Holistic View

## Purpose

- Maintain an integrated view of my life so decisions across areas stay aligned
  with the Spirit, my priorities, and reality.

## End State

- I have a clear, current picture of how each area is doing.
- Drift is detected early and addressed without panic.
- Tradeoffs are explicit and consistent with life intent.
- Reviews result in a small number of concrete decisions.

## Constraints

- Do not turn review into bureaucracy.
- Do not use review to avoid action.

## Tradeoffs

- Clarity over completeness.
- Truth over comfort.

## Risk tolerance

- High for experiments that improve clarity.
- Low for systems that add overhead without benefit.

## Escalation conditions

- Chronic confusion about priorities.
- Persistent drift across multiple areas.
- Conflicts between intents that block decisions.

## Verification steps

- A regular review happens and produces decisions.
- Reviews are short and sustainable.

## Activation / revalidation

- Active immediately upon adoption.
- Superseded when a new holistic-view intent is explicitly adopted.
- Revalidate quarterly or after major life changes.
