# INTENT: Bolt Foundry

## Purpose

- Build Bolt Foundry into a durable, product-led company that serves people
  well and compounds over time.

## End State

- Customers receive reliable, high-quality outcomes from Bolt Foundry.
- The company operates with clarity, integrity, and sustainable pace.
- Team members have clear priorities, healthy rhythms, and agency.
- The business model supports long-term resilience and growth.

## Constraints

- Do not compromise integrity for short-term wins.
- Avoid commitments that outpace capacity or focus.
- Protect time for deep work, product craft, and customer trust.

## Tradeoffs

- Focus over breadth.
- Durability over speed.
- Customer trust over vanity metrics.

## Risk tolerance

- Medium for experiments that improve product-market fit and resilience.
- Low for moves that increase fragility or erode trust.

## Escalation conditions

- Core priorities are unclear or shifting weekly.
- Customer trust or product quality degrades.
- Team health or pace becomes unsustainable.

## Verification steps

- Weekly review of priorities, delivery, and customer signals.
- Monthly review of product direction and operating cadence.
- Quarterly review of strategy, finances, and team health.

## Activation / revalidation

- Active immediately upon adoption.
- Superseded when a new Bolt Foundry intent is explicitly adopted.
- Revalidate quarterly or after major shifts in strategy.
