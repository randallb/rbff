# INTENT: randallb.com

## Purpose

- Maintain randallb.com as a clear, useful home for writing and updates that
  reinforce long-term intent.

## End State

- The site reflects current thinking and priorities.
- Posts are coherent, timely, and aligned with core intent.
- Drafts move to publication with minimal friction.
- The writing cadence is sustainable.

## Constraints

- Do not publish reactive or low-quality posts.
- Avoid scope creep beyond core themes and commitments.
- Protect writing time from operational churn.

## Tradeoffs

- Clarity over volume.
- Depth over timeliness.
- Consistency over novelty.

## Risk tolerance

- Medium for experiments in format or tone that improve clarity.
- Low for content that distracts from primary commitments.

## Escalation conditions

- The site drifts from current priorities.
- Drafts stall without a clear path to publish.
- Writing cadence becomes unsustainable.

## Verification steps

- Weekly review of draft pipeline and next post.
- Monthly review of themes and reader signal.
- Quarterly review of site direction and archive health.

## Activation / revalidation

- Active immediately upon adoption.
- Superseded when a new randallb.com intent is explicitly adopted.
- Revalidate quarterly or after major shifts in focus.
