# INTENT: Money

## Purpose

- Steward money to reduce stress, increase freedom, and support what the Spirit
  is asking of me.

## End State

- Monthly cash flow is clear and stable.
- Spending aligns with priorities and values.
- Savings and giving are consistent and intentional.
- Money supports freedom, generosity, and long-term stability.

## Constraints

- Do not take on avoidable debt.
- Avoid financial decisions that compromise integrity or relationships.
- Keep commitments within conservative, sustainable limits.

## Tradeoffs

- Stability over speed.
- Freedom over lifestyle inflation.
- Generosity over accumulation.

## Risk tolerance

- Low for speculation or high-volatility bets.
- Medium for calculated investments aligned with long-term goals.

## Escalation conditions

- Spending consistently exceeds planned limits.
- Financial stress begins to crowd out priorities.
- Money decisions conflict with conscience or values.

## Verification steps

- Weekly review of spending and cash position.
- Monthly budget reconciliation.
- Quarterly review of savings, giving, and long-term goals.

## Activation / revalidation

- Active immediately upon adoption.
- Superseded when a new money intent is explicitly adopted.
- Revalidate quarterly or after major financial changes.
