# Areas

Use this folder for ongoing responsibilities that do not end when a single
project ships.

## What Belongs Here

- spiritual life and holistic review
- close relationships and family responsibilities
- company responsibilities
- client relationships
- personal operations
- health, home, money, craft, learning, systems, and community
- durable facts and plans for each life area
- reference context that should survive task completion

## What Does Not Belong Here

- one-off efforts with a clear finish line; use an area `projects/` folder or
  root `../projects/`
- reusable references; use `../resources/`
- archived or inactive responsibilities; use `../vault/`
