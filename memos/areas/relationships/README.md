## Things

Use Things for check-in reminders and calls. Keep durable relational context,
priority order, and patterns here.

## Lightweight CRM

This area also holds lightweight relationship context. It is not intended to be
a complete CRM or contact database.

Use:

- [`people/`](people/) for durable person context.
- [`companies/`](companies/) for organization context.
- [`opportunities/`](opportunities/) for active or possible relationship-linked
  opportunities.
- [`notes/`](notes/) for one-off meeting prep or source notes.

Keep records small:

- who they are
- relationship/context
- current open loop
- last meaningful interaction
- relevant source links

Do not try to capture every email or keep this perfectly synced with Things,
Calendar, Gmail, or Granola. Things remains the execution layer.
