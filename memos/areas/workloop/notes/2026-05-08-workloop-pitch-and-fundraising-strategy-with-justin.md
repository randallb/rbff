## Core Capture

### Company Vision

- WorkLoop is the trusted way for agents and people to work together and trust each other.
- WorkLoop helps everyone know:
  - who is allowed to do what
  - who did what
  - how the process can improve
- WorkLoop is desktop-first software:
  - Mac today
  - all devices later
  - local/device-responsive software is framed as more trustworthy for autonomous work
- Security is a first-principles foundation:
  - passkeys
  - cryptographic security
  - host-layer access controls
  - future peer-to-peer relationships through cryptographic invites
- Everyone is a coworker:
  - people
  - agents
  - services
- Coworkers work together through the same basic model:
  - define tasks they can perform
  - define requirements for those tasks
  - create results that other coworkers can use
- Training an agent should mirror onboarding an employee:
  - do the job yourself
  - have the agent take over
  - watch it work
  - give feedback
  - let autonomy increase as trust increases

### Product / Operating Model

- Main unit of organization is an org chart.
- Initial owners are the founders.
- Chief of Staff is the primary generalist agent interface:
  - less authority than owners
  - likely the agent users chat with most often
  - handles unspecified work in the style of Claude Code or Codex
  - may hire other coworkers
- Internal WorkLoop/BF mono model:
  - `memos` stores company information and work
  - coworkers include Dan, Justin, Randall, and Chief of Staff
  - services include Slack, Linear, and other key systems
  - areas include fundraising, product development, customer work, sales, and marketing
  - specialized coworkers exist under areas, such as investor updates and pipeline management
- WorkLoop should be dogfooded for the fundraise:
  - executive assistant pulls relevant Granola notes
  - updates CRM / spreadsheet / Google Drive status
  - manages scheduling and follow-up

- OpenAI competition answer:

  - this is investor-palatable even if not the preferred outcome

### Future Product Direction

  - users create cryptographic invites

  - data exchange follows that relationship
  - person, agent, service, and computer are all coworkers in one social/operating system
- Long-term frame: operating system for social connection and AI-mediated collaboration.
